Who are we

Don Bosco Green Alliance Nigeria is a movement under and in-line with the Don Bosco Green Alliance international movement of young people from the Salesian Family institutions, who contribute to global environmental action, thought and policy.

Our Mission

To create an environment that is safe and caring for all life on the planet, while building up a new generation of environmentally committed citizens and leaders.
